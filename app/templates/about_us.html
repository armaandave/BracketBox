Who are we?

Bracket Box is a website that specializes in box office data and creating metrics for comparing and scoring movies. Along with standard box office statistics, we provide a custom score that takes into account risk and reward of a movie and its production.

What does our score signify?

To calculate our score, we combine a variety of different factors to value both the risk success of the performance of any particular at the box office. To calculate this, we collect vast amounts of data and use statistical algorithims to produce an accurate score.
