About Us

Read about who I am and what I do

  

My name is Armaan Dave and I am a student in the greater Seattle area. I love watching movies and I also love tracking how well certain movies are doing at the box office. So, I have created a place where you can view all the box office information you could possibly want. Along with this, I have created a custom scores that takes into account all the different factors of a movie (the budget, the gross, movies it was competing against, actors, etc) to create a custom score that indicates the total quality of that movie
